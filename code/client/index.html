    The easiest way to find your perfect bag!

    Here at BagCupid our aim is to help bag shoppers find the perfect bag. There is a sea of handbags and purses out there and though there are many websites offering these items for sale there is none like us. There are websites that allow you to filter but none offer the precision of BagCupid. It doesn’t matter if this is your first handbag or your 150th – we understand how hard it is to shop for handbags online sometimes and we want to make this task easier for you. We designed our website so that it is super easy and quick for you to apply the filters that will help you find the best bag. Simply start typing into the search box and you will see options populated right away. Click on the different tags to narrow down the perfect bag for your needs. You can narrow down your choice by brand, color, style, material, size, handles, straps and type.

    Notice the simple design of our website? There are no flashing banners and no annoying advertisements and pop-up screens. We are not into distractions – we are into the business of helping you find the right bag. We want you to leave our website with a conviction that you have chosen the right bag and not with frustration after going through handbag listings one after the other only to be more confused than you were in the first place. So we kept our design, simple and straight to the point. It is all about the handbags here.

    Not only does our website offer user friendly filtering but we also have a blog that is packed with informative content. The content on our blog can be helpful with choosing the right bag for an event, setting or based on what you intend to have it carry. Our blog also includes written pieces on various designers and the audience they cater to as well as the kind of line that they carry. Our blog is informative and is updated often with the latest and best information on handbags, their design and manufacture along with trends. No fluff is what we guarantee just clean cut informative pieces.

    We have it all. We educate you on handbags and provide the shopping marketplace where you can find the ideal handbag for your needs.

    BagCupid is the brainchild of Connie Zoltu; a self-professed handbag fanatic. She is a lover of handbag variety and as such knows the struggles of handbag shopping. She knows just how much specifics matter. In her very own words, she describes BagCupid:

    “It's like a dating website but rather than finding a soulmate, you find your dream bag.”
